--- title: Transjective tags: concept description: Transjective is a kind of relation that emerges between the subjective and objective through mutual interaction. ---

Transjective1 is a kind of relation that is neither purely subjective nor purely objective, but emerges between the subject and object through their mutual interaction and co-shaping.

The subjective/objective binary is insufficient for understanding things like meaning, relevance, consciousness, affordances, love, etc. These aren't just properties of the thing itself (objective), or something imagined by the human (subjective), but a living relation between them.
