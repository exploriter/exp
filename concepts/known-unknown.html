Known-Knowns Things we're aware of and understand.
We know that we know.
Known-Unknowns Things we're aware of but don't understand.
We know we don't know.
Unknown-Knowns Things we understand but are not aware of.
We know but don't know it's relevant.
Unknown-Unknowns Things we're neither aware of nor understand.
We don't know what we don't know.
