--- title: The Transcendentals tags: ["concept", "collection"] description: How the transcendentals — Truth, Goodness, and Beauty — relate to each other. ---

Beauty

Total coherence.

Unified, participatory, perception, emergent.
Beauty informs truth.

Truth

Outer coherence.

Cold, masculine, thought, relative.
Truth serves goodness.

Goodness

Inner coherence.

Warm, feminine, feeling, absolute.
Goodness orients to beauty.
