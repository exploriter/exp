--- title: Expertise, Experts, and the Expert Class tags: ["concept", "collection"] ---
Expertise The actual knowledge, skill, and insight acquired through rigorous, domain-specific study and practice. Real and enduring.
Expert A person who possesses expertise. (They may or may not be part of an institutional class).
Expert Class The socially and institutionally recognized stratum of people who are labeled, credentialed, and given authority as "experts" in public discourse. This is a socio-political role, not just (or even necessarily) a function of real expertise.
