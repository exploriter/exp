--- title: Triadic Loops tags: playground description: Triadic loops are closed, living cycles in which meaning is oriented, disclosed, articulated, and returned to lived experience. ---

An exploration of how the immanent, omniscient, and transcendent are encountered by the subjective using the axioms of The Immanent Metaphysics.

  • The Transcendent orients to the Immanent.
  • The Immanent informs the Omniscient.
  • The Omniscient serves the Transcendent.

Notation

Class has the connotation of context (a general whole), and instance has the connotation of content (a specific part).

  • Ic = class of the immanent
  • Ii = instance of the immanent
  • Oc = class of the omniscient
  • Oi = instance of the omniscient
  • Tc = class of the transcendent
  • Ti = instance of the transcendent

Foundational Triadic Loop Examples

Loop 1: Beauty, Truth, and Goodness

  • Beauty ↔ Immanent
  • Truth ↔ Omniscient
  • Goodness ↔ Transcendent

We'll start with beauty since it is the most fundamental (immanent) and loop back to it. Here is what the full loop looks like:

  • ...Goodness → Beauty → Truth → Goodness → Beauty...

And here is the same loop but adding in classes and instances:

  • ...Goodnessc → Beautyi ⇒ Beautyc → Truthi ⇒ Truthc → Goodnessi ⇒ Goodnessc → Beautyi...

And here is the same loop again but now fully abstracted using our notation:

  • ...Tc → Ii ⇒ Ic → Oi ⇒ Oc → Ti ⇒ Tc → Ii...

In English, this is just saying:

...a class of goodness precedes an instance of beauty (which implies a class of beauty), which precedes an instance of truth (which implies a class of truth), which precedes an instance of goodness (which implies a class of goodness), which precedes and instance of beauty...

Or, more verbose, but less abstract:

...the general concept of goodness always comes before a specific beautiful thing (which brings along with it the general concept of beauty), which always comes before a specific true thing (which brings along with it the general concept of truth), which always comes before a specific good thing (which brings along with it the general concept of goodness), which always comes before a specific beautiful thing...

Now let's actually proceed through the loop:

Step 0: Beauty as Immanent (Ii)

Since beauty is the perception of coherence, we start with its immediate encounter and disclosure. It is not yet explained. A moment, a face, an act, a landscape, a painting, a sentence...whatever it is, it just lands with rightness.

But wait! We know that this instance of beauty (immanent) must be preceded by a class of goodness (transcendent) as we might already be wondering, what makes this encounter recognizable as beauty at all?

Step 1a: From Goodness to Beauty (Tc → Ii)

Since goodness is what beauty (and therefore coherence) feels like from the inside, we can now properly start the loop with beauty since we now know how we were able to recognize it as such in the first place. Goodness, as the transcendent horizon of the possibility/recognizability of rightness, is why this particular instance of coherence shows up as beautiful.

Step 1b: The Immanent Class that Beauty Implies (Ii ⇒ Ic)

Encountering a specific instance of beauty implies there is a generalizable kind of thing here. The general recognizability of wholeness in perception. In other words, beauty as a class.

Step 2a: From Beauty to Truth (Ic → Oi)

Since truth is what beauty (and therefore coherence) looks like from the outside, we can begin to articulate coherence to capture some aspect of it. Maybe we percieve a painting as harmonious (instance of beauty), which implies harmony as a class, and then we can produce propositions like, "...it is harmonious because the proportions of such and such are balanced...," etc.

Notice that this truth is always selective and partial. Focussing on parts makes it immensely powerful, but that power is ultimately destructive if it loses connection to wholeness. And its only connection to the whole is when it is in service to goodness.

Step 2b: The Omniscient Class that Truth Implies (Oi ⇒ Oc)

Encountering a specific instance of truth, again implies there is a generalizable kind of thing here. Intelligibility-as-such. "Truth in principle," form, logic, lawfulness, the propositional space. In other words, truth as a class.

Step 3a: From Truth to Goodness (Oc → Ti)

What "ought" to be arises when a particular situation becomes intelligible in a way that discloses choice. It is the space where reason becomes real and there is a concrete call to do (or not do). You cannot be obligated by that which you literally cannot make sense of in some way.

Step 3b: The Transcendent Class that Goodness Implies (Ti ⇒ Tc)

Encountering a specific instance of goodness, once again implies there is a generalizable kind of thing here. The transcendent horizon of the possibility/recognizability of rightness, obligation, worthiness. In other words, goodness as a class.

Step 4/1a: From Goodness Back to Beauty (Tc → Ii)

The horizon of worthiness now tunes our attention, shapes what is salient, and trains our perceptions so that the immanent world shows up as a field of coherence that we can participate in and with. Beauty is perceived as beauty, and the loop continues into eternity.

And so, going back to the beginning, we can say:

  • Goodness orients to Beauty.
  • Beauty informs Truth.
  • Truth serves Goodness.

  • Vision ↔ Transcendent
