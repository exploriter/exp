--- title: Mindfulness Meditation tags: practice description: Mindfulness meditation is a practice that combines centering and rooting to train attention. ---

It's not about what happens in your sitting, it's about what happens from your sitting to your life. Don't hold your practice hostage to the feeling of a clear mind.

  1. Center your posture, attention, and attitude.
  2. Find your root.
  3. Continue to center your attitude and attention.
