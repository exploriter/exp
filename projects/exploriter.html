Exploriter is a digital knowledge garden and personal philosophical project. The name itself is a portmanteau of the explore/exploit tradeoff. Content is organized into two main layers:

  1. Knowledge Types: Concepts, Methods, and Stories
  2. Participation/Activity Types: Playgrounds, Projects, and Practices
