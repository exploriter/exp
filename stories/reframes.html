--- title: Fundamental Moves (Reframes) tags: story ---

There are some fundamental moves that when you can make them (and keep them), the world becomes a much bigger, brighter, and more coherent place. You become more aligned with reality.

  1. I am an individual → I am a person.
  2. We have relationships → We are relationships.
  3. Faith is belief without evidence → Faith is embodied and enacted belief.
  4. I'm feeling [lost|bored|??] → Seek novelty; make art; help out.
  5. Buy & Sell → Kindly use; fairly exchange; generously offer.

The term "reframe" is borrowed from the great Scott Adams.
